ABOUT LEA AND OUR MISSION

LEA stands for local, ethical and affordable. We're here to give students and community members in the Bay Area a resource to find stores and restaurants that fit around our motto. Why LEA? We, the founders, are students in the Bay Area and were inspired to create an outlet that we couldn't find. The idea first sprouted when we wanted to find local, ethical and affordable clothing/beauty stores all in one place, however, we stemmed to include more community aspects by exploring other things we enjoy—community events, volunteering and of course, discounts.
